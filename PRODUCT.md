# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
High-end device owners (iPhone, iPad, premium Android) looking for technical repair services. They require absolute professionalism, transparency, and expert care. Their context is diagnostic anxiety: they want to be assured that their expensive equipment is in clinical-grade hands.

## Product Purpose
Deliver an interactive landing page for FIXMI, featuring a high-fidelity 3D device showcase and apple-inspired scroll storytelling. It aims to present diagnostic repair services as premium hardware engineering rather than street-side swapping.

## Brand Personality
- Technical Precision (calibrated, lab-grade, sharp)
- Diagnostic Trust (clean, clinical, transparent)
- Understated Premium (solid metals, dark slate, glowing diagnostic orange `#f26a21`)

## Anti-references
- Neon Indigo-Cyan SaaS gradients (looks like a generic template)
- Terracotta/cream warmth (unsuited for clean room diagnostics)
- Generic orange construction/mechanical shop aesthetic (too cheap/rough)
- Overly rounded button pills (>12px) and glowing card borders
- Tracking Kickers (small uppercase eyebrows) on every section

## Design Principles
- "Hardware as Art" - treat device components with photographic, surgical clarity.
- "Diagnostic Contrast" - highlight only active parameters, pricing, and structural states in pure diagnostic orange tones against metallic slates.
- "Clinical Restraint" - visual depth through clean glass filters and raw metal values, not decorative drop shadows.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratios of at least 4.5:1 for body copy against the dark slate background.
- Respect prefers-reduced-motion media query for all transitions.
